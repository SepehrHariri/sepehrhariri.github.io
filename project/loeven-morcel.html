Loeven Morcel

The team at Loeven Morcel reach out to us because they needed to embellish their online appearance. They were also releasing new lines of furniture. They needed a website where they could sell that furniture, and also showcase their elegant work. This project would become the foundation for their visual identity going forward. So we reshaped the logo and overall aesthetic to turn it into something that truly speaks to their intended audience.
